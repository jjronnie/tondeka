About Horizon Trips and Tours

Welcome to Horizon Trips and Tours

Welcome to Horizon Trips and Tours, your gateway to unforgettable adventures across Africa.

With Horizon, embark on a journey that transcends conventional tourism, delving deep into the heart of Uganda's natural splendor and the captivating allure of other African destinations.

Our passion for exploration is matched only by our commitment to sustainability, as we strive to preserve the pristine environments and vibrant cultures that define this continent.

At Horizon, we offer meticulously crafted itineraries designed to showcase the diverse landscapes.

Discover abundant wildlife, and rich cultural heritage that make Africa truly unique.

Whether you dream of trekking through lush rainforests to encounter endangered mountain gorillas, embarking on thrilling safaris across the sweeping savannas in search of the Big Five, or immersing yourself in the rhythm of traditional African music and dance, we have the expertise and resources to turn your dreams into reality.

As pioneers in responsible tourism, we work closely with local communities and conservation organizations to ensure that our travels leave a positive impact on both the environment and the people we encounter along the way.

Our Services

Safari Tours

Embark on an unforgettable safari adventure through the iconic national parks and game reserves of Uganda and Kenya. Encounter the Big Five and witness the breathtaking landscapes of the African wilderness.

Cultural Experiences

Immerse yourself in the vibrant cultures and traditions of East Africa with our immersive cultural experiences. Meet local communities, participate in traditional ceremonies, and learn about centuries-old customs and rituals.

Gorilla Trekking

Experience the rare opportunity to trek through the dense forests of Bwindi Impenetrable National Park or Mgahinga Gorilla National Park in search of the majestic mountain gorillas. Witness these incredible primates in their natural habitat.

    Prominent Ugandan Tourist Destinations

    • Murchison Falls National Park

      Experience the power and beauty of the Nile River as it thunders through a narrow gorge at Murchison Falls, where visitors can enjoy game drives, boat safaris, and birdwatching.

    • Queen Elizabeth National Park

      Explore diverse ecosystems, including savannah grasslands and wetlands, and spot a variety of wildlife such as elephants, lions, hippos, and birds.

    • Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

      Home to half of the world's population of mountain gorillas, Bwindi offers unforgettable gorilla trekking experiences in its dense rainforests.
